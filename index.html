About the Mediterranean Hackathon

The Mediterranean Hackathon invites teenagers from across Egypt to join a day of environmental innovation. This event is completely free and welcomes all skill levels.

We will provide free food and snacks to keep participants energized throughout the day. Bring your laptop, charger, and enthusiasm for environmental solutions!
